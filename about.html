Hello!

I used to think that designing buildings was best way to have an impact on people’s lives through design.
However although it has given me a solid grounding in creative problem solving, I realised that digital product design would allow me a more direct connection with those I am designing for, while also tapping into my lifelong fascination with technology.

I have been studying and working in a design based discipline for the past 6 years and I use this experience to deliver designs that are thoroughly thought through and put the needs of the user first.

I'm currently looking for opportunities in a fast paced, exciting environment where I can help create digital experiences that are a joy to use.

If you’re interested in seeing what I got up to before I embarked on my digital product design journey then please click here.

When I’m not doodling or designing you’ll either find me up a mountain, seeking out the latest illustration exhibiton, tinkering inside a computer or attempting to teach myself Spanish (any tips welcome!)
